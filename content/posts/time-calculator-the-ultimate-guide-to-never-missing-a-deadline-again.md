If it's currently 3:00 PM, one hour from now is 4:00 PM on the same day.

**What is 2 hours 30 minutes from now?**

## Date Rollovers and Edge Cases (The Tricky Stuff)

Here's where automated calculators really prove their worth—handling all the annoying edge cases that trip up mental math.

### Crossing Midnight

Adding 5 hours to 10:00 PM doesn't give you 15:00 PM (that's not a thing). The **hours from now calculator** automatically knows:
- 10:00 PM + 5 hours = 3:00 AM *tomorrow*

### AM/PM Handling

The transition from PM to AM and vice versa is seamless:
- 11:00 AM + 2 hours = 1:00 PM (not 13:00 AM)
- 11:00 PM + 2 hours = 1:00 AM next day

### Today vs Tomorrow Logic

**24 hours from now** always means exactly one day ahead, same time:
- Today 3:00 PM → Tomorrow 3:00 PM

**48 hours from now** means two full days:
- Monday 2:00 PM → Wednesday 2:00 PM

### Month and Date Changes

The calculator handles:
- January 30 + 5 days = February 4
- December 30 + 5 days = January 4 (next year)
- Leap years and varying month lengths

No more forgetting that February only has 28 days or that April has 30.
